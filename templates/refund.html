POOJA RAJVIR BHATI – Cancellation & Refund Policy

1. Commitment to Customers

At POOJA RAJVIR BHATI, we prioritize helping our customers and ensuring a smooth shopping experience. To support this, we have implemented a liberal cancellation and refund policy designed to accommodate changes while maintaining product quality and service efficiency.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are considered only if the request is made within 6-8 days of placing the order. Please note:

  • If the order has already been communicated to vendors or merchants and the shipping process has begun, cancellation requests may not be entertained.
  • Perishable items, including flowers, food, and other time-sensitive products, cannot be cancelled once ordered.

3. Refunds & Replacements for Damaged or Defective Items

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please report it to our Customer Service Team within 6-8 days of receipt.

  • The merchant will verify the claim at their end before approval.
  • Refunds or replacements will be provided if the quality of the delivered product does not meet expectations.

4. Product Not as Expected

If a product received does not match the description on our website or does not meet your expectations, you must notify our Customer Service Team within 6-8 days of delivery.

After reviewing your complaint, the team will take an appropriate decision regarding replacement or refund.

5. Manufacturer Warranty Products

For products that come with a manufacturer’s warranty, any issues should be referred directly to the manufacturer as per their warranty terms.

6. Refund Processing

Once a refund is approved by POOJA RAJVIR BHATI, it will take approximately 6-8 days for the amount to be processed and reflected in the customer’s account.
